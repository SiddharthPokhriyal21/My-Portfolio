Experience

Winner of Anxiety Arena, DAKSHAM 2.0: The Management Fest

I secured the highest rank among 100+ participants in Anxiety Arena, a stress event in DAKSHAM 2.0, an inter-college event held in March, 2024 in our university campus, organized by the School of Management at Graphic Era Hill University, Dehradun.

The event comprised of three rounds:

  • Round 1: Logical Reasoning Written Test
  • Round 2: Extempore Speaking
  • Round 3: Stress Interview
Crowdfunding internship at Muskurahat Foundation

I served as a crowdfunding intern where I successfully completed a fulfilling one-month crowdfunding internship with Muskurahat Foundation in August, 2023. I raised a fund of ₹1500 to support underprivileged children's access to quality education.

CODZILLA 3.0: A Competitive Programming Contest

I took participation in CODZILLA 3.0, the Competitive Programming Contest under "AGAAZ", the Winter TechFest held on 16th December - 17th December, 2022 in the Graphic Era Hill University, Dehradun Campus, organized by the Co-Dev Community (the coding club at Graphic Era University).

The contest aimed to test our competitive programming skills and was conducted in two rounds. I cleared the first round with ease. Although the second round was difficult for me.

HACK-O-HOLIC: A 24-Hour Hackathon

I took participation in HACK-O-HOLIC - a 24-hour hackathon held on 11th November - 12th November, 2022 at the Graphic Era Hill University, Dehradun Campus, organised by the Co-Dev Community (the coding club at Graphic Era University).

It was really an enriching experience as the starting of my coding journey. We were a team of 4 which was supposed to create a website in 24 hours. I played my part as the frontend developer of the team. Our topic was "Enhancing Tourism in Uttarakhand" for which we developed a beautiful frontend project. The judges were impressed with our frontend development.
